Privacy
Policy

At Paassy, protecting your personal data is at the heart of our commitment to providing you with the best event experience.

Data Collection

We collect only the information necessary for the proper functioning of our ticketing service:

  • Identity & Contact: Your name, email address, and phone number to send your tickets and verification codes.
  • Booking Information: Details regarding your reservations, ticket types, and event history to ensure seamless access.
  • Location: With your consent, to suggest events near you.

Cookies and Tracking

Paassy uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary to maintain your session and cart.
  • Analytical Cookies: To understand how users interact with our platform.
  • Preferences: To remember your language and theme choices.

Data Usage

Your data is used to:

  • Generate your unique digital tickets (QR Codes).
  • Provide customer support and manage reservations.
  • Send important notifications regarding your events.
  • Prevent fraud and ensure transaction security.

Data Retention

We keep your information as long as necessary to:

  • Provide requested ticketing services.
  • Comply with legal and tax obligations.
  • Resolve disputes and enforce our agreements.

Sharing & Security

Paassy is committed to protecting your privacy. Your information is shared only in the following cases:

  • Organizers: For ticket validation at the event.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law to protect our rights or public safety.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, rectify, and delete your personal information. You can exercise these rights directly through the app settings or by contacting us.
